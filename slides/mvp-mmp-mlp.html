Minimum viable product - Minimum to fulfill requirements of solving the problem. "the smallest thing you can build that lets you quickly make it around the build/measure/learn loop"
Minimum marketable product - Minimum to be a marketable solution to the problem. Something to shout about
My little pony, no! (Minimum loveable product - Smashing Mag) - Minimum needed for user to say/think "I love this!" "The version of a new product that brings back the maximum amount of love from your early tribe members with the least effort."
